About me

I’m one of those ”inspired by Turner” artists and I’m not ashamed of it. He has been my favourite artist since I was 15… Probably because of the way he shows light in his paintings… Ever since I found out about him, I’ve been trying to recreate the feeling in my own paintings, not in the same way and not even a similar way, but in a way that’s unique to my own style. Right now I’m still searching for the most effective way to show it. Some of my paintings show more light than others, some are admired by people and some are hated by me and hidden in my room, but all of them are a part of my journey, if not as an artist, then at least as a painter.
